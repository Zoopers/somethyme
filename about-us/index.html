Who we are.

The Beginning of Thyme

Some Thyme was started in Philippi in 2008, and in 2009 the company moved to bigger premises in Maitland. We opened a satellite shop in Sea Point in 2011, where we are now very well established. Our vehicles can be seen there every day of the week.

Our Thyme Line

Eventually we outgrew our Maitland premises and moved to Epping, a hop and jump away from the market. All orders big or small are treated with the same level of importance. Prices and quality are tailored to each customer’s needs. We believe in ethics and have strong principles – a small coffee shop cannot be expected to buy the same quantity as a large restaurant, but they still require the same quality of fresh produce. We pride ourselves on giving the best quality and value for money. Stock is rotated regularly, and the stock that can no longer be sold is donated to shelters or soup kitchens.

Thyme For Quality

Yoram has over 25 years of experience in the industry and is always ahead of the curve. Yoram has the ability to read the market very well and is able to buy the best quality available at the right price. He has extensive experience in setting up the display for the retail trade. Yoram has built a fantastic reputation based on honesty and reliability. His word is his bond.

Our product list is constantly growing. If we do not stock it, we will source it for you! We have friendly staff who are always willing to go the extra mile.
